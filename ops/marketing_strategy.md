# Marketing Strategy

## Keyword Strategy
Prioritize long-tail urgent queries, city plus service searches, problem-intent pages, cost guides, FAQ queries, and comparison/decision content.

## Local SEO
Use Dallas-Fort Worth service-area relevance without fake addresses, fake offices, fake Google Business Profiles, fake reviews, or fake license claims.

## AEO
Every important page includes a direct answer, question-based headings, emergency steps, cost guidance where relevant, FAQs, internal links, and a clear CTA.

## Outreach
Use safe foundational citations, partner outreach, supplier relationships, property manager outreach, and PR angles. Do not use spam backlinks, PBNs, paid ranking-passing links, or automated mass submissions.
