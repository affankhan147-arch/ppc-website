# Image Safety Policy

Do not copy competitor images.
Do not use fake job-site photos as real.
Do not use fake customer photos.
Do not use fake technician photos.
Do not use fake before-and-after photos.
Use generic diagrams, icons, licensed stock with source tracking, or real partner photos with documented permission.

Every image must be tracked in `ops/image_asset_tracker.csv`.

Day 3 public visuals use original SVG diagrams and abstract service-area graphics only. They do not show technicians, customers, job sites, licenses, badges, reviews, office locations, or partner claims.
