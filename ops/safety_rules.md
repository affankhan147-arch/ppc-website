# Safety Rules

Do not create paid ads, Google Ads, Bing Ads, fake Google Business Profiles, fake addresses, fake reviews, fake licenses, fake insurance claims, fake local office claims, fake technician photos, fake customer photos, copied competitor content, copied competitor images, spam backlinks, PBNs, or paid ranking-passing links.

Do not store passwords, API keys, GitHub tokens, OpenAI keys, payment details, or private credentials in repo files.

Use honest lead-generation wording and owner-approved claims only.
