# Project Blueprint

## Goal
Build a reusable pay-per-call local lead-generation software platform. Version 1 launches emergency plumbing and drain cleaning in Dallas-Fort Worth.

## Active Market
- Vertical: emergency plumbing and drain cleaning
- Market: Dallas-Fort Worth, Texas
- Future market: Phoenix Metro as inactive configuration only

## Lead Types
- Phone call
- Form lead
- Future chat lead

## Lead Buyers
- plumbers
- contractors
- agencies
- aggregators
- restoration partners

## Revenue Model
- pay per qualified call
- pay per qualified form lead
- future monthly exclusive sponsorship

## Quality Rules
- minimum call duration threshold
- duplicate filtering
- market and service match
- daily buyer cap
- fallback routing
- no lost lead when one buyer is unavailable

## KPIs
impressions, indexed pages, organic clicks, calls, forms, call conversion rate, qualified calls, revenue per call, buyer acceptance, and pages passing QA.
