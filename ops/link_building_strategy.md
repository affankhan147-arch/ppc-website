# Safe Citation And Link Building Strategy

## Allowed
- foundational citations
- local and home-service directories
- supplier outreach
- property manager outreach
- restoration partner outreach
- useful PR/content angles
- tracked natural anchors

## Blocked
- spam automation
- mass comment spam
- paid ranking-passing links
- PBNs
- fake business listings
- fake reviews

Track all links in `reports/backlink_tracker.csv`.
