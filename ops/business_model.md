# Business Model

The platform generates organic inbound calls and form leads, then routes them to qualified buyers based on market, service, buyer status, cap, payout, and fallback rules.

The first revenue model is pay per qualified call. Future models can include pay per form lead and exclusive monthly sponsorships.

The site must use honest provider-connection wording. It must not claim to be a licensed plumbing company, local office, insured contractor, or reviewed provider unless the owner supplies proof.
