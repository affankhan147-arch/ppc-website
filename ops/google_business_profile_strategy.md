# Google Business Profile Strategy

No fake Google Business Profile may be created.

GBP can be used only for a real eligible business or real partner contractor. If a buyer or contractor has a real GBP, future partner landing pages and attribution links can be created after owner approval.

For now, the site uses organic SEO pages, safe service-area content, citations where appropriate, and buyer outreach. Do not use a fake address, virtual office, fake staff, or false service-area claims.
