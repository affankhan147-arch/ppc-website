# SEO + AEO Strategy

## Page Architecture
- homepage
- 10 core service pages
- 16 Dallas-Fort Worth city pages
- 160 city plus service pages
- 8 problem pages
- 5 cost guide pages
- 30 blog posts
- FAQ hub
- contact, partner, privacy, terms, and disclosure pages

## Metadata
Every public page should have a unique title, meta description, canonical URL, visible H1, CTA, internal links, and useful content.

## Schema
Use Organization, WebPage, Service, BreadcrumbList, and FAQPage where appropriate. Do not use fake ratings, review schema, aggregateRating, fake LocalBusiness address, or fake GBP claims.

## Internal Links
Connect services, cities, city-service pages, problems, cost guides, and blog posts. Use natural anchors, brand anchors, URL anchors, and location-natural anchors.
