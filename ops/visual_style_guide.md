# Visual Style Guide

The site should feel urgent, trustworthy, utilitarian, and conversion-focused.

## Palette
- slate for authority and readability
- orange for primary request actions
- cyan accents for water, service-area, and emergency context
- white and mist backgrounds for readable dense content

## UI Rules
- clear phone CTA
- mobile sticky call bar
- no fake photos
- no decorative stock-style imagery
- use icons, diagrams, and structured content where safer than photos
- avoid exposing internal business strategy on public pages
- keep city pages transparent about service-area coverage
