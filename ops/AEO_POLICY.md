# AEO Policy

Answer Engine Optimization means every important page must answer urgent user questions clearly enough for AI summaries, featured snippets, FAQ discovery, and voice-style emergency searches.

## Required Page Elements
- direct answer near the top
- emergency CTA
- question-based H2 or H3 headings
- what-to-do-now steps
- when-to-call guidance
- cost factors where relevant
- local service-area guidance where relevant
- 5 to 8 useful FAQs where appropriate
- FAQ schema where FAQs are present
- internal links
- final CTA

## Quality Rules
- answer quickly before long explanation
- use clear short paragraphs
- do not keyword stuff
- do not invent fake authority
- do not claim guaranteed response time
- do not claim license, insurance, office, review score, or ratings unless the owner supplies proof
- do not create low-value AI spam pages
