## What this plan will NOT produce, stated honestly

No specific call-volume number (any plan claiming "10,000-15,000 calls" in
60 days without citing methodology is fabricating that number - legitimate
SEO does not work on a guaranteed schedule). What this plan will produce by
Day 60: a technically healthy, fully-indexed site with genuine content depth
across the full grid, a small number of real durable backlinks, verified
analytics and call tracking, and zero penalty/deception risk - a foundation
that keeps compounding in month 3, 6, and 12, rather than needing to be
abandoned and rebuilt.
