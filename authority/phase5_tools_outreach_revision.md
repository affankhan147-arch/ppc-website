# Phase 5 Revision — Legit Tools + Outreach Plan (Replaces Scrape/Blast Version)
# Added: revision of the original 45-60 Day Roadmap's Phase 5 (Local Authority and Links)
# Applies to both PlumbingHands.com and UrgentHomePros.com

## Why this revision exists

An external "15-Day Sprint" plan was proposed (TDLR-scraping tools, 1,200 cold
emails in 6 days, mass Reddit/forum posting, gated PDF reports blasted to news
outlets). It conflicts with this project's own GLOBAL NON-NEGOTIABLE RULES
(no automated backlink submissions, no forum spam, no manipulative link
exchanges) and carries real external risk independent of those rules:
Yelp/Angi scraping violates their Terms of Service, and 1,200 emails in 6 days
from an unwarmed domain risks the sending domain's deliverability reputation
for real customer emails too. This revision keeps the good ideas (free tools,
real content, industry reports) and replaces the risky mechanics with slower,
durable alternatives.

## Phase 1 — Real Tools (replaces scraped-data tools)

- License Verification Guide (per site/niche): deep-links into TDLR's own
  official public search (does not scrape it), wrapped with genuine
  educational content (what license types mean, red flags to check, how to
  verify). Legit, embeddable, no ToS or legal exposure.
- Cost Calculator tools (plumbing + roofing): no data-sourcing risk, build
  as originally planned.

## Phase 2 — Real Content (same intent, real sourcing)

- Blog posts / guides built the same way as the Property Manager Plumbing
  Emergency Triage Sheet (genuinely useful, real research, no fabricated
  stats) — already proven this session (3,518 words, real checklist content,
  verified live).
- Industry report per niche: sourced from actual public data (TDLR public
  stats, Census/BLS, insurance industry reports), properly cited. Not
  scraped, not blasted cold to news outlets.

## Phase 3 — Curated Outreach (replaces mass cold-email + forum spam)

- Hand-picked, researched targets only (property managers, restoration
  companies, trade associations) — same method used for the 5 DFW property
  manager pitches drafted this session (Flat Fee Landlord, CW Sparks, JoGip,
  Westrom Group, RPM Meridian).
- Realistic cadence: 5-10 genuinely personalized pitches per WEEK per site,
  not per day. A daily recurring prospecting task is already scheduled
  (see: Daily backlink/guest-post prospecting) to surface new researched
  targets automatically.
- Real HARO usage: respond to live journalist queries matching the niche.
  Reactive, not cold-blasted.
- Trade associations: individual, researched asks to Texas plumbing/roofing
  associations — not templated mass submissions.
- Reddit/forum presence, if used: individual, genuinely helpful participation
  in relevant threads over time, not bulk cross-posting the same link to
  many communities in a short window (this pattern gets flagged by
  auto-moderation regardless of content quality, and most such links are
  nofollow anyway — limited SEO value even if not removed).

## Phase 4 — Compounding (ongoing)

- Every new page/guide gets IndexNow-pinged (already working on both sites).
- Each outreach success becomes a reference point for future pitches.
- Monthly: track ACCEPTED-CALL REVENUE from any traffic these efforts
  produce, not raw backlink count — consistent with the original roadmap's
  own stated success metric (Section 1: "optimize for accepted revenue per
  visitor, not page count").

## Honest expected outcome vs. the original 15-day plan

Original plan claimed 40-100 backlinks in 15 days via volume. Realistic
expectation with this revision: 5-15 genuine, durable backlinks in the same
window, growing steadily after — because these links come from real
relationships and real utility rather than mass submission, they don't get
pruned by moderators, flagged as spam, or ignored at a 99%+ rate the way
cold-blast outreach typically is.
