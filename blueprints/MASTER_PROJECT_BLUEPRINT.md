# Master Project Blueprint

## Reusable Platform
Build local lead-generation sites from data files, not one-off hard-coded pages.

## Version 1
- Vertical: emergency plumbing and drain cleaning
- Market: Dallas-Fort Worth
- Goal: organic calls and form leads through useful SEO + AEO content

## Reuse Steps
1. Choose a vertical.
2. Choose a market.
3. Update services, cities, problems, cost guides, buyers, FAQs, and content templates.
4. Keep safety and compliance rules.
5. Run QA and reports before launch.
