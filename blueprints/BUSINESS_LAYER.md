# Business Layer

## Business Model
Generate qualified inbound calls for service categories through organic web pages, helpful content, and clear call routing.

## Revenue Path
The platform should eventually support pay-per-call style monetization, but no payment, ad, or buyer contract setup belongs in this first workflow phase.

## Business Rules
- Organic-first until owner approval changes the strategy.
- No fake local offices or unverifiable claims.
- No fake reviews, fake licenses, or fake insurance claims.
- Calls should be generated through useful, accurate, compliant content.

## Owner Decisions Needed Later
- target verticals
- target service areas
- call tracking provider
- partner or buyer qualification rules
- monetization terms
