# Marketing Layer

## Positioning
Helpful, direct, service-area focused pages that answer urgent customer questions and encourage calls.

## Channels
- Organic search
- AEO and answer snippets
- Helpful local/service content
- Future owner-approved partnerships

## Blocked Channels For Now
- Paid ads
- Google Ads
- Bing Ads
- Spam backlinks
- Fake review campaigns

## Content Principles
- answer the user's urgent question quickly
- show service-area relevance without fake office claims
- avoid copied competitor media
- use original or licensed assets only
- make call paths obvious and honest
