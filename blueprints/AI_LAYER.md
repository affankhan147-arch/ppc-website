# AI Layer

## AI Tool Roles
- ChatGPT: planner, strategist, command creator.
- Codex: implementation, testing, fixes, reports.
- Colab: support lab for generated files and analysis outputs.
- AI agent role cards: consistent specialist behavior.

## AI Safety
AI tools must not invent business credentials, reviews, offices, licenses, insurance claims, or addresses. They must not create paid ads or spam links.

## Expected AI Outputs
- commands
- page briefs
- keyword files
- outlines
- implementation reports
- QA summaries
- owner checklists
