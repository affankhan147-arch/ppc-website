# Future Vertical Expansion

## Expansion Concept
The workflow should support additional lead generation verticals after the first PPC platform foundation is proven.

## Candidate Future Verticals

## Expansion Rules
- Verify legal and licensing requirements before publishing claims.
- Do not create fake local presence.
- Do not reuse competitor assets.
- Build vertical-specific content and QA checklists.
- Keep each vertical documented in blueprints before implementation.
