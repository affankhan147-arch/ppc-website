# Day 2 Session 1 Selected Pages

## Selection Method

The audit reviewed 77 money pages from built HTML and source data: homepage, contact page, primary services, high-value problems, cost guides, city pages, and controlled city-service pages. Search Console, ranking, traffic, and conversion data are NOT AVAILABLE IN REPOSITORY, so selection used repository evidence: commercial intent, urgency, current content depth, CTA path, schema, internal-link support, and ability to improve without unsupported claims.

## Selected Page 1

- URL: /services/24-hour-emergency-plumber
- Page type: Primary service page
- Primary intent: Emergency service selection
- Business value: Highest-intent emergency plumbing entry point
- Main weakness: Needed richer emergency decision framework, provider-check expectations, and DIY stop guidance.
- Reason selected: It had high commercial or emergency intent and could be improved with truthful decision support, safety guidance, FAQs, and internal links.
- Planned improvement: Added emergency decision support, diagnosis preparation, DIY boundaries, prevention guidance, extra FAQs, and links into burst-pipe, toilet-overflow, and cost resources.
