# Day 3 Premium Design Proposal For Owner Approval

Date: 2026-07-12

## Direction

Upgrade Plumbing Hands into a premium emergency plumbing request website for Dallas-Fort Worth. The public experience should feel fast, trustworthy, service-area transparent, and conversion-focused.

## Approved Public Positioning

- Emergency plumbing help across Dallas-Fort Worth
- Request urgent plumbing help
- Get connected with available plumbing help
- Find help for urgent drain, sewer, pipe, toilet, and water-heater problems
- Service availability may vary by location, timing, and provider coverage

## Design Choices

- Use a navy, cyan, orange, white, and mist palette.
- Use original abstract SVG diagrams instead of copied competitor photos.
- Use direct service cards, city links, cost guides, FAQs, and a prominent form.
- Avoid claims about offices, credentials, reviews, prices, or guaranteed arrival.

Implemented in repo for review. No paid ads, citation submissions, review generation, or new indexing submissions were performed.
