# Day 2 Internal Linking Report

## Linking Rules Applied

- Homepage links to main services, priority city pages, problem pages, and cost guides.
- Service pages link to related problem pages, related cost guides, and controlled city plus service pages.
- City pages link to main service pages, nearby city pages, and controlled city plus service pages where applicable.
- City plus service pages link back to the city page, main service page, related problems, and related cost guide.
- Problem pages link to recommended service pages and related cost guides.
- Cost guides link to related service pages, problem pages, and contact.

## Safety

Internal links avoid fake local-office claims and avoid excessive repeated exact-match anchor text.

## Controlled City Plus Service Links

Only 16 priority city plus service combinations are generated and linked as primary local landing pages. This avoids unlimited doorway-page scaling.
