# Conversion Design Gap Report

Date: 2026-07-12

## Gaps Found

- Homepage needed a stronger first-screen emergency request path.
- Placeholder phone number needed a safer CTA fallback.
- Form needed clearer service, city, urgency, name, phone, and optional message flow.
- Public pages needed less internal language and more provider-availability disclosure.

## Improvements Completed

- CTA defaults to request form while phone is still a placeholder.
- Hero includes urgent problem framing and provider coverage disclosure.
- Form now includes service selection, city or ZIP, urgency, contact fields, optional message, and safer microcopy.
- Footer repeats the service availability disclosure.

## Remaining Conversion Inputs

- Real tracked phone number.
- Approved provider/CRM endpoint.
- Analytics IDs in hosting environment variables.
- Optional real partner photos only after documented permission.
