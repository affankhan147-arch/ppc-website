# Remaining Owner Inputs

## Required Before Public Growth Push

- Real owner-approved call tracking number
- Confirmed call tracking provider, if any
- Real buyer routing endpoints or CRM destination
- Approved buyer list with real eligibility, coverage, hours, caps, payout, and minimum call duration
- Owner-approved lead storage policy
- Production analytics IDs, if desired
- Google Search Console verification after Day 1 QA is accepted
- Bing Webmaster setup after Google is complete

## Still Deferred

- Email setup
- IndexNow
- citation submissions
- backlink outreach
- paid ads
- Google Business Profile

## Claims Requiring Owner Proof

Do not add these without real proof:
- license claims
- insurance claims
- physical office claims
- verified review claims
- real technician/customer/job-site images
- before/after photos
- response time guarantees

## Ready For Day 2 After Approval

Day 2 can start once the owner accepts Day 1 QA. Day 2 should scale SEO + AEO content, internal links, FAQs, problem pages, cost guides, and content QA without starting Google/Bing indexing or outreach yet.
